# **App Name**: Toquero Football Academy

## Core Features:

- Academy Presentation: Introduce Gaizka Toquero's football academy with its mission, vision, and values.
- Training Programs and Schedules: Display available training programs, schedules, and age groups.
- Coaches Team: Showcase the coaching team with their profiles and expertise.
- Contact and Registration: Provide a contact form and registration details for new members.
- SEO Optimization Tool: Generate a SEO audit report using the contents of the page and make improvement suggestions. The tool will assess title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, keyword usage, internal and external links, image optimization and mobile friendliness.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Grass green (#BF6083) to evoke the feel of the football field.
- Background color: Off-white (#0D0D0D) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Red (#73D9D9) for a dynamic, sporty feel.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif for impactful headings; Body font: 'PT Sans' to ensure readability and modernity.
- Layout inspired by abetxuko.360player.club: Clean, immersive design focused on clear calls to action (CTAs).
- Use sport-themed icons representing different academy aspects and training types.
- Subtle transition animations for improved user experience, following a minimalist approach.
