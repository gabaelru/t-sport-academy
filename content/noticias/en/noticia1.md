Toquero Sport Academy, is established in Vitoria to promote sport in our city.

Our main mission is to train young people in the passion for sport, developing their sporting skills, whatever their level, and to promote the values that sport offers us.

All this in a close environment, with innovative tools and a fully qualified team.

Through this App you can be informed of all activities, calendars, developments and administrative procedures related to Toquero Sport Academy.
